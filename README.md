AI Expense & Business Tracker
🤔 What is this project?
This is a smart, AI-powered web application that helps people track their daily expenses and income. Instead of filling out long, boring forms like traditional accounting apps, this project uses Artificial Intelligence (AI) to do the hard work. You can simply type a sentence like "I paid 2,500 rupees for electricity today" or upload a picture of a shopping receipt, and the AI will automatically understand the amount, category, and date.

👥 Who is it for?
The project is designed with two specific types of users in mind:

For Families & Individuals (Household Mode): It helps regular people track their monthly salary, utility bills, groceries, and savings so they don't overspend.

For Small Businesses (Business Mode): It helps local sellers, dropshippers, and small e-commerce setups track their daily revenue, advertising costs, inventory purchases, and net profit.

❓ Why was it built? (The Problem it Solves)
Most financial tracking apps fail because people get tired of manually typing every single detail. The main goal of this project is to save time and remove the friction of data entry. By using advanced AI (like Gemini and Groq), the app acts like a personal digital accountant. You just talk to it or show it a bill, and it organizes everything perfectly.

✨ Key Features & How It Works
Smart Text Entry: Just write what you spent in plain English or Roman Urdu, and the AI extracts the exact numbers and categories.

Receipt Scanner: Upload an image of an invoice or restaurant bill, and the AI vision technology will read the text from the image automatically.

Live Google Sheets Sync: The app connects directly to the user's Google Drive. Every transaction is safely saved into a beautiful, auto-generated Google Sheet in real-time.

Visual Dashboard: The app features a beautiful dashboard with charts and cards that show total income, total expenses, and profit/loss history at a glance.

Overspend Alerts: If a user spends more than they earned in a billing cycle, the system immediately shows a warning alert.
